First, a bit of background:

A Canadian art rock music group from Montreal.

Sunset Rubdown is powered by the seemingly limitless Spencer Krug, also known for his role in Wolf Parade, amongst others. Formed in 2005, their most recent release was in 2009.

The band’s sound is highlighted by distinct drum, keyboard, and guitar signatures; their live shows are almost unrivaled.
